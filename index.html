About

I build AI applications with clear workflows and measurable behavior.

My work is grounded in software engineering practice: define product scenarios, design service state and APIs, connect retrieval and tools, then use harnesses, traces, and tests to keep behavior reproducible. I focus on making agent systems maintainable rather than only impressive in demos.
